The History

Dogs were first domesticated over 15,000 years ago, becoming the pets we love over the next few thousand years. Dogs are descendants of the wolf, and have changed in different ways over the years, resulting in the many different dog breeds. Pugs were first mentioned by Confucius in 551 BC.

The Evolution

Obviously, dogs all for the most part looked like wolves, though this changed in the following millennium. Many smaller dogs such as the pug may be cute, but they are as far from a normal dog as you can get. Many small dogs are the result of inbreeding for specific deformities within the breed.
